Three confidential mandates from the last quarter.

A CEO navigates a cross-border acquisition before external advisors are engaged.
Before triggering regulatory disclosure or briefing an investment bank, the member convened a Mandate with three chairs who had navigated the same target market. The conversation shaped the deal structure, the approach to the target board, and the regulatory sequencing, in a single closed session.

A family-office principal prepares to accept an external chairmanship.

Boardroom by EMIR convenes quarterly, closed-door, under House Rules. Between sessions, members meet through private briefings, roundtables, and bespoke events. Four times a year in person; continuously, peer-to-peer, in between.

Recent Events
Three sessions from this year's calendar.
Boardroom · Dubai
A minister's transition from public office to private capital.
Navigating the transition from senior government to private capital requires a quality of counsel that few advisors can provide. A Boardroom by EMIR session convened four former ministers who had made comparable transitions, covering regulatory exposure, reputational positioning, and the first ninety days in the private sector.
The member entered the private sector with two anchor relationships and a clear jurisdictional strategy, six months ahead of their original timeline.
Boardroom · Riyadh
A closed-door session on the future of sovereign capital deployment in the GCC.
Convened under House Rules with eight chairs and senior sovereign fund executives, the session explored how capital allocation mandates were shifting in response to geopolitical realignment. No agenda was published. No attribution was permitted.
Three bilateral conversations initiated at the session led to active mandates within sixty days.
Boardroom · London
Senior leaders from across the MENA diaspora convene on cross-border investment.
A quarterly Boardroom session brought together twelve senior decision-makers operating between London and the Gulf, family-office principals, former ministers, and sector chairs, to discuss deal flow, regulatory change, and market positioning in a closed-door setting.
Two investment introductions and one advisory mandate were initiated directly from the session.

Recent Deals
Three transactions formed inside the network.
GCC · Series C
Financing round for the region's No.1 fintech company.
A member seeking Series C capital for the region's leading fintech requested a Mandate. Within the network, three sovereign wealth fund principals and two family-office CEOs were convened, all with direct exposure to the sector. The conversation shaped the terms before a single investment bank was briefed.
The round closed at the member's target valuation. Lead relationships were formed inside the network, not through an advisor.
