EMIR is a private membership network founded in Dubai in 2011 by Trevor McFarlane. Our members are chairs, chief executives, ministers, and family-office principals. They meet four times a year, in confidence, to discuss the decisions they can't discuss in public.

Between meetings, members draw on private briefings from our analysts, peer counsel from one another, and an investor circle that surfaces deals before they reach the market.

When everyone else has a stake in the outcome, you need people who have none. That is what EMIR is for.
