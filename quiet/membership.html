Membership is invitation only or by referral.

We do not advertise openings and do not publish a roster.

Members are typically chairs, chief executives, ministers, senior officials, and family-office principals. They lead institutions with international exposure, carry decisions that touch policy or capital markets, and want counsel from peers who have faced the same call.

Members use EMIR for the decisions they can't take in public. A chief executive wants to know how peers handled a similar regulatory shift before announcing their own response. A minister wants candid feedback on a draft policy from people outside their administration. A board needs to discuss succession before it leaks. A principal needs an honest read on a deal before naming it.

In each case we bring together a small group of members who have faced the same question, sometimes joined by an outside expert. The conversation is private, the advice is unconflicted, and we don't publish what is discussed.

Membership includes:

  • peer advisory, under confidentiality
  • private intelligence briefings from our analysts
  • a quarterly meeting, currently held in Dubai
  • an investor circle for pre-market dealflow
  • bespoke services on request: executive search, master classes, media training

If a current member has suggested you write, we'll respond in confidence.
