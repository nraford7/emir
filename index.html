The Origin
From the Middle East's most influential network, Boardroom by EMIR, to a global network of leaders.

Trevor McFarlane founded EMIR on a single conviction: that the quality of a decision is determined by the calibre of the people in the room, and the rigour of the intelligence they act on. Today, we harness the power of the EMIR network by connecting our members with peer-led intelligence, research, strategic counsel, and investment access when they need it.

Led by Managing Partner Dr. Noah Raford, our Senior Advisors and Core Team include sitting and former chairs, CEOs, ministers, senior government officials, energy economists, geopolitical risk specialists, counterterrorism and intelligence practitioners, digital transformation leaders, and sector specialists spanning finance, energy, defence, media, and technology, across every major jurisdiction.

Drawn from some of the world's most influential organisations, among them SAMI, PepsiCo, Deutsche Bank, Accenture, Cisco, Honeywell, Reckitt, BAE Systems, GE, Northrop Grumman, Barclays, Procter & Gamble, Pfizer, Expo 2020 Dubai, Raedon, Dentons, Dubai and Abu Dhabi Government.
